How It Works

Join thousands of farmers and buyers who are transforming agricultural trade across Africa.

1

Register

Create your profile as a farmer or buyer and verify your account through our simple onboarding process.

2

List Products

Farmers list available produce with quantity, quality, and harvest dates for interested buyers.

3

Get Matched

Our algorithm matches farmers with buyers based on specific requirements and location proximity.

4
